About

Who are we?

I am Polina Kravtsova. I provide relocation and administrative support for people who are moving to Switzerland or already building their life here.

My path to Switzerland included studying, navigating residence procedures, and building a life here with my family. I started alone, without knowing a language. Because of this, I understand how complicated the system can feel when you arrive in a new country.

Together with swiss legal education and work in a migration-focused law firm here, this experience allows me to provide practical support, clear guidance, and accessible help at fair prices for people starting their journey in Switzerland. This is that i needed in the beginning and which i did not get. My help is affordable and clear.

FAQ

Find answers to your most common questions

Clear information about consultations, support, and the scope of assistance.

Is the first consultation free of charge?
Yes. The first consultation is free of charge. During this initial conversation we briefly analyse your case and discuss the possible next steps.
Do you provide legal representation?
No. I provide structured guidance, administrative support and document preparation. Where formal representation is required, I can refer clients to licensed lawyers.
Are consultations available online or in person?
Both options are available. Consultations can take place online or in person in Zürich depending on your preference.
Do you only work with migration matters?
Migration and relocation matters are the main focus. In some cases related administrative questions can also be addressed, while specialised areas may require referral to a qualified lawyer.
